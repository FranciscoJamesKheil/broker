Broker | House and Lot Price Prediction (Zamboanga City)

Having a house and lot property will give you an assurance to have a safe place to stay for you and your family. Globally, the number of homeowners around the world increases each year. In the Philippines, owning a house and lot property is a privilege for every Filipino. Locally, the closer the house and lot property is bought near a commercial area, the higher is the price rate and if bought far from the commercial area would have a lower price rate. This study aims to predict the estimated price of a house and lot using Linear Regression algorithm.
